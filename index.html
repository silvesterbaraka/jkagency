About JK Consultancy

JK Consultancy is a trusted educational and medical consultancy that builds strong bridges between Africa and India. Our expertise spans university admissions, healthcare coordination, visa processing, and personalized assistance—ensuring excellence at every step.

Mission: To provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality education and healthcare opportunities for African clients.

Vision: To become the leading consultancy connecting Africa with India’s academic and medical excellence.

Why Choose JK Consultancy?

We are committed to empowering African students and patients with trustworthy, ethical, and expert support for their academic and health journeys in India.
